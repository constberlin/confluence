Confluence Storytelling

a collective for transatlantic reporting founded by Constantin Berlin and Ian Strahn in 2022. Through our work, we reveal untold stories of people and places existing beyond the usual confines of transatlantic coverage. Our goal is to contribute to intercultural understanding between the United States and Germany by exploring socio-historical themes which shape both countries. We are using our transatlantic biographies and expertise to cover the transatlantic world and the points of confluence that connect it..
